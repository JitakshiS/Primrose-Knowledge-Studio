Changed · 05
Honey → Garnet. Personal Safety is heavy material, domestic situations, harassment, knowing when to involve law enforcement. Honey reads warm and sunny; that's the wrong emotional signal. Garnet is a jewel-tone deep red that reads protective and serious without alarming. Distinct from Clay's bright orange-red (different temperature, deeper saturation, no overlap in use).
