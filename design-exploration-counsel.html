For Jitakshi · the honest comparison

Would this pass the “different person” test?

Yes. Nothing visual carries over: new mark, new type, new palette, new pillar system, new page structure, new voice register. Side by side with the current Porch, these read as two different studios.

What Counsel changes at the identity level

  • Mark: the five-colour dot stack becomes a monochrome column glyph, five bars forming one pillar. Reads institutional, works at 16px, embosses beautifully.
  • Type: Instrument Sans at 500-650 weight replaces Outfit 900. Premium brands whisper; the size does the work, the weight stays calm.
  • Palette: ink navy, bone, one brass accent. Brass carries the legal-seal association and nobody in the space owns it.
  • Pillars: colour-coding removed entirely. A brass-numbered index reads like the contents of a carefully drafted document.
  • Structure: the page is chaptered like a story. Gap, library, counsel, terms, epilogue. Narrative order rather than section order.
  • Trust devices kept from Plain Sight: framed product UI, reviewed dates, credential facts, exit-on-the-label.

What adopting it would mean

  • The locked Pillar Stack system gets retired: Tailwind tokens, favicon, OG image, and all five member surfaces would be rebuilt in this identity. Roughly a week of rework.
  • The five-colour navigation logic inside the Fortress needs a replacement wayfinding system (numbers + position instead of colour).
  • TikTok-first cold traffic trade-off remains: this converts skeptics and readers; the current Porch punches harder in a feed screenshot.
  • Brass/ink photographs beautifully for her content: dark study, warm lamp, brass details. The brand and her filming setup could actually match.
